About G-Fest 2026

G-FEST is a University Expo organized to provide high school students with the opportunity to gain comprehensive information about various universities and higher education institutions, covering study programs, admission requirements, and available facilities. Through an interactive and informative atmosphere with exhibition booths from numerous universities, students can explore diverse educational options and understand the advantages of each institution. This is expected to help them make more informed and appropriate decisions in choosing their higher education path, aligning with their interests, needs, and future aspirations.
